Computer Networking and Computer Systems

Computer systems refer to the combination of hardware and software that work together to perform computing tasks. This includes components like processors, memory, storage, and operating systems. Computer networks, on the other hand, involve the interconnection of multiple computers or devices to facilitate communication and resource sharing. Networks enable data exchange and collaborative activities, connecting individuals and systems over various distances, from local setups to global infrastructures like the internet.
